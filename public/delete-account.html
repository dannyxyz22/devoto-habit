Account Management

Delete Account

Last updated: January 20, 2026

Overview

This page describes how you can request the deletion of your account and associated data in the Ignis Verbi app.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Option 1 — Through the app: Open the app → User menu (top right corner) → "Sign out" to log out. Then send an email requesting deletion.
  2. Option 2 — By email: Send an email to legatuschristi@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request - Ignis Verbi".
  3. In the email, provide the email address used to create your account in the app.
  4. You will receive confirmation within 7 business days.

Data That Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

Data Type Action Timeline
Account credentials (email) Deleted Within 30 days
Reading progress Deleted Within 30 days
Reading goals and plans Deleted Within 30 days
Statistics (streak, minutes read) Deleted Within 30 days
Physical book bookmarks Deleted Within 30 days
User preferences/settings Deleted Within 30 days
Error logs (anonymous) Retained for 90 days Deleted after 90 days
⚠️ Warning: Deletion is irreversible. After deletion, it will not be possible to recover your reading progress, streaks, or goals. Data stored locally on your device (cache, downloaded EPUBs) must be removed manually by uninstalling the app.

Retention Period

After requesting deletion:

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately.
  • Data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Anonymous error logs (without personal identification) may be retained for up to 90 days for app improvement purposes.
